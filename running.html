Life on the Track

From 5Ks, to laps on the track, running has formed me into who I am today!

My Story

I started running in middle school for fun. In sophmore year of high school, I had completely fallen in love with it. there is nothing better than an early morning run to get miles in or even after school to run off the stress of the day. I have competed at the state level along with the national level.

Since coming to college, I have stopped running ompetitively for now and I'm just enjoying the journey. Finding sactuary in every step. It's my time for myself and the Lord. Provo has some great trails, and I have loved running up the canyon.

How I Train

A solid training week looks different depending on the season. For now I just run for sanity sake. But this is what an in season week would look like.

  1. Monday — Run + Strides
    • 40min + Strides
  2. Tuesday - Speed Workout
    • Warm-up: 2 miles
    • Workout: 8x200m @ race pace w/ 90sec rest
    • Cool-Down: 2 miles
  3. Wednesday — Run
    • 40min
  4. Thursday — Mixed Energy Workouts
    • Warm-up: 2-mile warm up
    • Workout: 2x1k @ tempo, 4x400m @ 90sec
    • Cool-down: 2 mile
  5. Friday — Easy Run
    • 40min
  6. Saturday — Long Run
    • 8–10 miles at conversational pace
  7. Sunday — Rest Day
